Account & Data Deletion Request

Nexfutbol devs – Data Deletion Policy

If you are a user of any Nexfutbol devs application, you have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

📌 How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to:
📧 Nextfutbol8@gmail.com

Subject line:
Account Deletion Request

Include the following information:

  • Your app name (e.g. 2+ Odds Daily Tips)
  • Your email used in the app (Google account email)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Option 2: In-App Request (if available in future updates)

You may request account deletion directly from the app settings once this feature is enabled.

🗑 What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (name, email, UID)
  • Login data (Google account authentication reference)
  • App activity history (if stored)
  • Preferences and saved settings

⏳ Data Retention

  • Most data is deleted within 7–30 days
  • Some records may be retained for legal or security purposes where required

🔒 Important Notes

  • Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some anonymized data may remain for analytics purposes
  • This process applies to all Nexfutbol devs applications
